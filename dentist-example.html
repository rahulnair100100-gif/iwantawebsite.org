Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straight teeth. No metal. No one notices.

Invisalign uses a series of custom, clear plastic aligners to gradually move your teeth into the perfect position. Most treatment plans are complete in 12–18 months — and the aligners are removable, so eating and brushing stay completely normal.
